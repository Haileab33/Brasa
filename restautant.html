Our Story

Born from fire,
rooted in tradition

Brasa began as a dream shared between two chefs on opposite sides of the world — a vision of cooking that strips everything back to its most primal, honest form. Fire. Smoke. Patience.

We source exclusively from farms within a 150-kilometre radius, from producers we know by name. Each dish is a conversation between season and flame, plated with quiet confidence.
